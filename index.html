Follow the Yellow Spiral Path

Starting on the left, follow the yellow path with your cursor, until you reach the green square in the centre.

If your cursor wanders into the blue it's sudden death so take care!

Please make sure your browser has loaded the JavaScript before starting.

In Chrome, you may need to click the little red alert symbol to the right of the web address bar and select 'load unsafe scripts'.

To start a new game, please reload the page!
